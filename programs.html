Frequently asked questions
— What programs are available for international students in Gautam Buddha Nagar?
Gautam Buddha Nagar offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines like Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech), Business (BBA, MBA), Law (LLB, LLM), Design, Architecture, Media, Nursing, and more at universities like Gautam Buddha University, Sharda University, Amity University, and others.
— Are the programs taught in English?
Yes, the majority of programs offered to international students are taught in English. This includes lectures, study materials, and exams to ensure accessibility and global academic standards.
— What is the admission process for international students?
International applicants typically apply through the university’s international admissions portal. Required documents usually include academic transcripts, a valid passport, English proficiency test scores (like IELTS/TOEFL if applicable), and a statement of purpose. Each university may have slightly different requirements and timelines.
— Are there scholarships available for international students?
Yes, many universities in Gautam Buddha Nagar offer scholarships or fee concessions for meritorious international students. Some also have partnerships with governments or global education foundations that provide financial aid.
— What is student life like in Gautam Buddha Nagar?
Student life in Gautam Buddha Nagar is vibrant and diverse. The area has modern campuses, well-connected metro access, affordable accommodation, rich cultural heritage, and a growing tech and business ecosystem. International students can also enjoy extracurricular activities, cultural fests, and student clubs across campuses.
